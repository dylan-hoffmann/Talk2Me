BARNARDO. says  Who’s there?

FRANCISCO. says  Nay, answer me. Stand and unfold yourself.

BARNARDO. says  Long live the King!

FRANCISCO. says  Barnardo?

BARNARDO. says  He.

FRANCISCO. says  You come most carefully upon your hour.

BARNARDO. says  ’Tis now struck twelve. Get thee to bed, Francisco.

BARNARDO. says  Have you had quiet guard?
